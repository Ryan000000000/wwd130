Are you ready for a relaxing day filled with water and sunshine? Then our Truckee River Float trip is for you! This five hour float down the river is perfect for families and for those who just want to kick back and relax on the water. Along the way, you will discover quiet swimming holes, and soft sand bars that are perfect for a picnin lunch. Take a break from the valley heat and come enjoy a day on the water!

For the more adverntureous rafters, we offer trips down the South Fork of the American River. This trip includes Class I through Class III rapids which add a bit of excitement to the trip without being overwhelming. Raft through rocky gorges and enjoy the incredible scenery. Maybe even catch a glimpse of the many wildlife that live in the area. This is the perfect trip for those who are new to rapids and are looking for a bit more excitement!

If you are a thrill seeker, you will love rafthing the North Fork of the American River with it's class IV and V rapids! This is our most challenging trip and, while not for the faint of heart, it is also our most scenic. With both challenging rapids and beautiful scenery, this trip has the best of both worlds!
