Home page

Welcome to my resume. I hope that you will find it useful. Let me tell you a little bit about myself. I live in Newcastle, California. I am 21 years old, and am the oldest of three children. I have one brother and one sister. I have Aspergers Syndrome. I work best in quiet environments with little distractions. I am a very linear thinker and I that is why I enjoy subjects like math, science, and computer programing, because they are very straight forward and do not involve a lot of abstract thoughts. I am a very dedicated worker and always try to do my best. I am very reliable. I am currently serving on a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I am a service missionary, so I still live at home with my family, but I go out during the day to serve with different organizations throughout the Sacramento, California area. I will complete my mission service in June of 2023. I would like to one day become a computer programmer because I will most likely get to work in a quiet environment and, as mentioned above, I enjoy computer programming because it is very direct. I also enjoy helping others accomplish their goals. As part of my mission, I have really learned to love serving people and helping them out when they need it. I feel a great sense of accomplishment when I help people.
